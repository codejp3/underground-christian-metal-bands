BIO

When thinking about black metal and blackgaze, there are two things that usually do not come to mind, Jesus and Texas. But those are two things that came together to inspire Daygraves.
After moving back home to Texas, Travis joined the worship team at his church. He was spending his afternoon practicing for his Sunday's services and his evening listening to bands like Deafheaven and Alcest.
Eventually, it seemed only natural to try and blend the best of those worlds. That's when the idea for Daygraves began to grow.
Eventually the vision became clear. Loud guitars. Fierce vocals. Blast beats. Lyrics that are unashamed about Jesus and the truth of His word. The result of this experiment was Silver.
